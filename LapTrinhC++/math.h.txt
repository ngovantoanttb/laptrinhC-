-- <math.h> trong C chua cac ham toan hoc thong dung
**Mot so ham trong thu vien <math.h>
- acos(double x) - Tra ve gia tri arccos cua x
- asin(double x) - Tra ve gia tri arcsin cua x
- atann(double x) - Tra ve gia tri arctan cua x
- pow(double x, double y) - Tra ve gia tri x^y	
- sqrt(double x) -> Tra ve gia tri can bac 2 cua x
- ceil(double x) -> Tra ve gia tri nguyen nho nhat lon hon hoac bang x
- abs(double x)  -> Tra ve gia tri tuyet doi x
- floor(double x) -> Tra ve gia tri nguyen lon nhat nho hon hoac bang x
